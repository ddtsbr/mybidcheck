Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what we don't do with it. We'll keep it plain English.

1. What we collect

When you use MyBidCheck, we may collect the following: your email address (if provided), the quote document or text you submit for analysis, your general location (used to calibrate regional pricing data), and basic usage data like which pages you visit.

We do not collect your payment information directly. All payments are processed by Stripe, which has its own privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy.

2. How we use your information

We use your submitted quote solely to generate your analysis report. We use your email address only to deliver your report and, if you opt in, to send occasional updates about the service. We use location data only to improve the accuracy of regional pricing estimates in your report.

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data. Ever.

3. Who we share your data with

We do not share your quote data with contractors, third-party marketers, or anyone else. Period.

We use a small number of trusted service providers to operate the business — including Stripe for payments and email delivery services. These providers access only the data necessary to perform their specific function and are bound by confidentiality agreements.

4. How long we keep your data

We retain your submitted quote and report only as long as necessary to deliver your report and handle any support requests. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing support@mybidcheck.com and we will remove it promptly.

5. Your rights

You can request a copy of the data we hold about you, ask us to delete your data at any time, or opt out of any email communications. Just email us at [email protected] and we'll take care of it promptly.

6. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to redact any sensitive personal information (like your full address or Social Security number) from quotes before submitting.

7. Children

MyBidCheck is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact

Privacy questions or requests? Email us at [email protected]. We respond within 2 business days.
